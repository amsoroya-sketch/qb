## CONCLUSION

This specification provides **three complementary views** of word-level grammatical analysis:

1. **Tabular View** - Best for comparing multiple words side-by-side
2. **Hierarchical View** - Best for understanding sentence structure and relationships
3. **Card View** - Best for focused study of individual words

All three views display the **7 essential grammatical properties** requested:
1. POS (Part of Speech)
2. Gender
3. Number (Singular/Dual/Plural)
4. Definiteness (Ma'rifa/Nakira)
5. I'rab (Case)
6. Case Sign (Fatha/Kasra/Damma)
7. Murakkab (Compound Structure)

This creates a **comprehensive, user-friendly grammatical analysis system** that serves learners from beginner to expert level.
